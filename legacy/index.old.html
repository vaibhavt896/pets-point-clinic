About The Clinic

Six Years of Healing,
One Paw at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Ratanlal Nagar, Kanpur, Pet's Point Animal Clinic has been a beacon of trust for pet owners since its founding. Our philosophy is simple: every animal deserves the same compassionate, expert care that you'd want for your own family.

From routine wellness checks to life-saving emergency interventions at 3 AM, our team of experienced veterinarians and empathetic staff are always here. We don't just treat pets — we partner with families in their journey.
